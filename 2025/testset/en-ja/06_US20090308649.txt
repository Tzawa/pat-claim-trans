5. A method of signal transmission on a printed circuit board with reduced signal distortion, the printed circuit board comprising:
one or more layers of non-conductive substrate upon which are disposed conductive pathways that conduct signals, the signals characterized by distortion at least partly caused by orientation of the conductive pathways on the layers of the printed circuit board, and a periodically patterned reference plane, the periodically patterned reference plane comprising a conductor having discontinuities arranged in a periodically recurring pattern on a surface of a layer of the printed circuit board;
