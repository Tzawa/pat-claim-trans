1. A seat reclining device comprising:
	a lower arm held on a seat cushion side;
	an upper arm supported rotatably by the lower arm and held on a seat back side;
	a lock mechanism that regulates rotation of the upper arm relative to the lower arm;
	a cam disposed between the lower arm and the upper arm so as to actuate the lock mechanism; and
	a shaft that is engaged with the cam, wherein
	the lock mechanism includes two poles which are arranged symmetrically with respect to an axis of the shaft, and move in a radial direction of the shaft by contact with the cam so as to regulate or allow the rotation of the upper arm relative to the lower arm,
	the shaft has, on an external surface thereof, a plurality of grooves or flat surfaces extending in an axial direction, side surfaces of the grooves or the flat surfaces forming six or more parallel flat surface portions,
	the cam has a hole having a flat portion that comes into contact with the flat surface portion of the shaft, and having a diameter of a reference circumferential portion set to be larger than an outer diameter of the shaft,
	the flat surface portion of the shaft and the flat surface portion of the cam are formed in parallel with a line connecting contact portions between the two poles and the cam, and
	relative movement of the shaft and the cam along the line connecting the contact portions is allowed, while mutual rotation of the shaft and the cam is regulated.
