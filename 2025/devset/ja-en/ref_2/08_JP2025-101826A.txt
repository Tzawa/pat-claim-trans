1. An elevator cooperation system for causing an autonomous traveling mobile body to cooperate with an elevator, comprising:
	inside an elevator car, an in-car auxiliary operation unit for operating an in-car operation unit; and an identification mark for identifying an individual elevator from a remote location;
	outside the elevator car, an out-of-car auxiliary operation unit for operating an out-of-car operation unit; and
	at portions other than the elevator, a destination reception means for receiving a instruction about destination of the autonomous traveling mobile body; a boarding floor arrival detection means for detecting arrival of the elevator to a boarding floor including which elevator has arrived in a case where a plurality of elevators are present; an alighting floor arrival detection means for detecting arrival of the elevator to an alighting floor after the autonomous traveling mobile body has boarded the elevator; a boarding/alighting determination means for determining completion of boarding or alighting of the autonomous traveling mobile body on the elevator; and a drive command means for remotely issuing a drive command to the out-of-car auxiliary operation unit and the in-car auxiliary operation unit at the time of boarding or alighting from the elevator on a destination route.
