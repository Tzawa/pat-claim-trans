6. A deck installation method comprising:
	at a construction site of the deck, combining resin mats in a required length in a longitudinal direction relative to a laying surface, and connecting the resin mats by connecting means to be arranged;
	similarly connecting and arranging the resin mats in a lateral direction, and coupling the resin mats also in the lateral direction by the connecting means; and
	placing deck materials thereafter on the resin mats corresponding to their length, and aligning their positions such that engaging projections of the resin mats are fitted into engaging holes of the deck materials,
	whereby the deck materials are disposed on the laying surface, and a deck is formed.
