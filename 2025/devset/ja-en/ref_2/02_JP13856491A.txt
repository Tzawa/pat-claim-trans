A sewing machine automatic feed control device comprising: upper and lower fabric feed means for feeding two fabrics respectively under a needle; sewing means for sewing the two fabrics fed under the needle; upper and lower fabric feed amount detection means for detecting the feed amounts of the upper and lower fabrics moving under the needle by the fabric feed means; feed amount control means for controlling relative feed amounts of the upper and lower fabrics to a desired set value by feedback of detection values from the upper and lower fabric feed amount detection means; and a seam allowance control means for controlling the seam allowance to a constant or arbitrary value, wherein the sewing machine automatic feed control device comprises upper and lower fabric elongation correction means for correcting an error due to elongation included in the feed amounts detected by the upper and lower feed amount detection means by inputting data on elongation of the fabrics in a sewn fabric, the data being estimated from characteristic data on elongation for each inclination angle with respect to the grain of the fabric and inclination angle data with respect to the grain of the sewing line, and the upper and lower fabric feed means are controlled and driven by the upper and lower elongation correction means to sew with a desired tuck ratio.
