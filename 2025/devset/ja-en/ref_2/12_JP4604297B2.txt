A seat reclining device comprising a lower arm held on a seat cushion side, an upper arm rotatably supported by the lower arm and held on a seat back side, a lock mechanism for regulating rotation of the upper arm relative to the lower arm, a cam disposed between the lower arm and the upper arm to operate the lock mechanism, and a shaft engaged with the cam, wherein the lock mechanism includes two poles arranged symmetrically with respect to an axis of the shaft and configured to move in a radial direction of the shaft by contact with the cam to regulate or allow rotation of the upper arm relative to the lower arm, the shaft has a plurality of grooves or flat surfaces extending in an axial direction on an outer surface of the shaft, side surfaces of the grooves or the flat surfaces forming six or more parallel flat surface portions, the cam has a hole having a flat surface portion in contact with the flat surface portion of the shaft and having a diameter of a reference circumferential portion set larger than an outer diameter of the shaft, the flat surface portion of the shaft and the flat surface portion of the cam are formed parallel to a line connecting contact portions of the two poles and the cam, and the seat reclining device is configured to allow relative movement of the shaft and the cam along the line connecting the contact portions while regulating rotation of the shaft and the cam.
