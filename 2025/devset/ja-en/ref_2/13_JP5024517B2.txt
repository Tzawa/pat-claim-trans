1. A thermosetting polyamideimide resin composition (characterized by) comprising:
(1) a polyamideimide resin having a carboxyl group and/or an acid anhydride group at a molecular end, or a polyamideimide resin having a carboxyl group and/or an acid anhydride group at a molecular end in which the molecular end group is optionally capped with an end-capping agent; and
