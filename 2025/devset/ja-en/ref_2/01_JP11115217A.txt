A negative pressure booster comprising: a housing defining at least one pressure space therein; a movable wall disposed in the housing to be movable forward and backward with respect to the housing, the movable wall dividing the pressure space into a front chamber and a rear chamber, the front chamber being connected to a negative pressure source, the rear chamber being selectively connected to the front chamber and the atmosphere; a power piston coupled to the movable wall; an input member disposed in the power piston to be movable forward and backward with respect to the power piston, the input member being movable by an operation of an operation member; a valve mechanism including a negative pressure valve configured to connect the rear chamber to the front chamber in response to movement of the input member, and an atmosphere valve configured to connect the rear chamber to the atmosphere in response to movement of the input member; and an output member configured to be moved forward by forward movement of the power piston in response to movement of the movable wall, the output member being configured to output a forward force of the power piston to the outside of the booster, wherein the negative pressure booster further comprises an actuator capable of generating a forward force in the output member independently of a forward force generated in the power piston when the valve mechanism is operated by an input to the input member accompanying an operation of the operation member, wherein an output from the output member is adjustable in response to a driving force of the actuator, and wherein the output member is configured to return to an initial position by a reduction in the input to the input member when the actuator is operated.
