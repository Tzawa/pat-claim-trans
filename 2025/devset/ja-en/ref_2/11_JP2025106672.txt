A washing machine comprising: a main body having an opening for loading and unloading an object to be washed into and out of a washing chamber, the opening being open toward a front side; and a door portion fixed to the main body so as to be rotatable between a closed position in which the opening is closed and an open position in which the opening is open, wherein the door portion includes a door main body that covers the opening when the door portion is in the closed position, a door holding portion that is rotatably fixed to the main body and protrudes from the door main body toward the washing chamber when the door portion is in the closed position, and a roller portion that is rotatably provided on the door holding portion, wherein the main body is provided with a plate spring having a portion formed with a locking portion that locks the roller portion that moves in accordance with rotation of the door holding portion in the closed position, and wherein assuming that a movement direction of the roller portion when the door portion rotates from the open position toward the closed position is a closed direction, the plate spring is disposed such that a fixed portion fixed to the main body is located closer to the closed direction than the locking portion.
